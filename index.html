Who Am I?

Currently I am an unemployed student at Università di Padova, studying Computer Science. Before university I attended high scool, studying mechatronics, mostly focused on mechanic at A. Malignani in Udine, Friuli Venezia Giulia, Italy.

In order to attend my studies I live in Padova center, on a nice position near all the necessary university headqarters, supermarkets etc. As so I can move between the apartment and my house in the north east of Italy, thus permitting me to be present in Friuli and in Veneto depending on the necessities.

At Malignani the main things I got teached about where:

  • Mechanical Dimensioning of objects
  • CAD drawings on Autocad Inventor. I also started learning myself about Fusion360 for my 2 3D printers.
  • Hand drawing of mechanical objects.
  • Physical and mechanical tests on samples following the standards ISO (UNI EN 10027).
  • Iron-Carbon phase diagram, and other phase diagrams.
  • Various processes for iron based alloys, such as thermal treating, nitruration, cementation, laser/waterjet/electroerosion cutting, ultrasounds for crack detection etc.

Writing as you can see by this website is not one of the things I am good at nor I like doing more than necessary, I do it just enough to be accurate on what I am describing, as opposed to using computers for all the amazing things they are capable of, especially discovering all the things you can use them for, that on a first look you would not be able to directly see, but only by using them and discovering new problems you search for new solutions and progress in understanding better the machine you work with.

What are my interests?

I get fascinated by newly discovered things, I especially like researching new things such as solutions to problems, features for programs/webpages/applications. All the things I do not know are generally only steps away for me to start searching them for interest or to find a solution.

I have currently worked on many things, the main branches where mechanical and computer science in general.

What about my free time?

On my free time I like riding my motorbike, doing kitesurf, skiing, snowboarding and working on my 3D printers to improve them; if you have my same interests we might meet to share them!
