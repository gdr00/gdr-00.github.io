Who Am I?

I am a student at Università di Padova, pursuing a master’s degree in Computer Science. I earned my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Università di Padova, completing it on July 19, 2024. For details about the competencies, criteria, and my final results, you can refer to the official badge . Prior to university, I studied mechatronics at A. Malignani in Udine, Friuli Venezia Giulia, Italy, with a focus on mechanical engineering.

I currently live in the heart of Padova, centrally located near university facilities, supermarkets, and other essential services. This convenient location allows me to easily travel between my apartment and my family home in northeastern Italy, providing flexibility to be present in both Friuli and Veneto as needed.

During my time at A. Malignani, I acquired knowledge and skills in the following areas:

  • Mechanical dimensioning of objects
  • CAD design using Autodesk Inventor. I also explored Fusion 360 for personal projects with my 3D printers.
  • Hand-drawing mechanical components
  • Conducting physical and mechanical tests on materials, following ISO standards (e.g., UNI EN 10027)
  • Understanding phase diagrams, including the iron-carbon phase diagram
  • Learning various metalworking processes such as thermal treatments, nitriding, carburizing, and advanced cutting methods like laser, waterjet, and EDM. I also studied techniques like ultrasound crack detection.

Writing, as you might notice from this website, is not my strongest skill nor something I particularly enjoy doing. However, I aim to write just enough to convey my ideas clearly and accurately. My real passion lies in exploring the incredible capabilities of computers, uncovering new ways to use them, solving unexpected problems, and discovering innovative solutions. This process not only enhances my understanding of the technology I work with but also fuels my enthusiasm for learning and experimentation.

What Are My Interests?

I am fascinated by new discoveries, especially when it comes to researching innovative solutions to problems or exploring new features for programs, operating systems, and applications. Anything I don’t yet know is simply a stepping stone for me to dive deeper, whether out of curiosity or to find practical solutions.

My work spans a variety of areas, with a primary focus on mechanical engineering and computer science in general.

What About My Free Time?

In my free time, I enjoy kitesurfing, scuba diving, riding my motorbike, skiing, snowboarding, and working on my 3D printers to enhance their capabilities. If you share any of these interests, I’d love to connect and exchange ideas!
